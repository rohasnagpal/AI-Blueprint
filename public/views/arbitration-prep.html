Arbitration Prep

Generate a structured arbitration preparation report from indexed matter documents.
How it works

Arbitration Prep organizes arbitration materials into a lawyer-reviewed preparation report. It does not decide the dispute, provide legal advice, or predict the award.

Upload
  • Notice or request for arbitration
  • Answer, response, or counterclaim
  • Statements of claim and defense
  • Contracts and arbitration clause
  • Procedural orders and timetables
  • Key correspondence and exhibits
  • Witness statements or transcripts
  • Expert reports and damages schedules
Output
  • Case snapshot and procedural posture
  • Issues for determination
  • Factual chronology and evidence matrix
  • Witness and cross-examination prep
  • Procedural tasks and hearing deadlines
  • Damages and remedies analysis
  • Strengths, weaknesses, and uncertainty points
  • One-page hearing preparation plan
Leave blank to use all indexed documents in the selected matter.
