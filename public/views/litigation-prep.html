Litigation Prep

Generate a structured litigation preparation report from indexed matter documents.
How it works

Litigation Prep organizes court materials into a lawyer-reviewed preparation report. It does not decide the dispute, provide legal advice, or predict the outcome.

Upload
  • Complaints, petitions, answers, and replies
  • Counterclaims and affirmative defenses
  • Key contracts, policies, or instruments
  • Court orders, schedules, and deadlines
  • Discovery requests, responses, and productions
  • Correspondence and notices
  • Witness statements, transcripts, and affidavits
  • Expert reports, damages schedules, and exhibits
Output
  • Neutral case summary and case snapshot
  • Claims, defenses, issues, and proof elements
  • Factual chronology and evidence matrix
  • Discovery, witness, and deposition prep
  • Motion, argument, and trial themes
  • Cross-examination topics
  • Procedural tasks, deadlines, damages, and remedies
  • Risks, gaps, and one-page litigation plan
Leave blank to use all indexed documents in the selected matter.
