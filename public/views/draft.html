Draft

Generate contracts, notices, letters, resolutions, and memos as printable HTML.
How it works

Draft uses an agentic workflow to turn your instructions and optional source documents into structured legal text.

  • Specialized agents identify document type, parties, facts, jurisdiction, tone, and required clauses.
  • They assemble a printable draft and preserve source context where matter documents are selected.
  • The workflow produces a starting draft for lawyer review, revision, and final approval.
Choose the drafting posture. Use formal or neutral for most legal documents; use plain-language or client-friendly when readability matters most.
Select matter documents the draft should rely on. Leave blank when you want the draft based only on your instructions.
