Mediation Prep

Generate a neutral, private mediator preparation report from indexed matter documents.
How it works

Mediation Prep is a private preparation tool for mediators. It does not decide the dispute, predict the outcome, or replace the mediator.

Upload
  • Mediation briefs
  • Pleadings or claim documents
  • Contracts, policies, or agreements
  • Party correspondence
  • Invoices, claim forms, or payment records
  • Prior settlement offers
  • Expert reports or supporting documents
  • Court or arbitration orders, if any
Output
  • Neutral case summary and factual chronology
  • Key legal, factual, commercial, and emotional issues
  • Party positions vs underlying interests
  • BATNA/WATNA and possible ZOPA
  • Strengths, weaknesses, uncertainties, and risk allocation
  • Settlement levers and information gaps
  • Caucus questions, impasse points, and bridge proposals
  • Private prep note and one-page session plan
Leave blank to use all indexed documents in the selected matter.
