Negotiation Prep

Generate a structured negotiation preparation report from indexed matter documents.
How it works

Negotiation Prep organizes matter materials into a lawyer-reviewed negotiation plan. It does not recommend settlement, decide the dispute, or predict outcomes.

Upload
  • Pleadings, demand letters, or position papers
  • Contracts, policies, or commercial terms
  • Correspondence and negotiation exchanges
  • Invoices, payment records, and damages schedules
  • Prior offers, counteroffers, and term sheets
  • Authority documents and internal constraints
  • Expert reports or valuation support
  • Confidentiality terms and procedural orders
Output
  • Neutral negotiation summary and posture
  • Positions, interests, concessions, and proof gaps
  • Chronology and evidence matrix
  • Information gaps and authority questions
  • BATNA/WATNA, leverage, and risk points
  • Offer framing and settlement option themes
  • Reality-testing questions and talking points
  • One-page negotiation session plan
Leave blank to use all indexed documents in the selected matter.
