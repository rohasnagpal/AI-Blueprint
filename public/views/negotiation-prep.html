Negotiation Prep

Prepare structured negotiation work product from indexed matter documents.
How it works

Negotiation Prep uses an agentic workflow to convert matter materials into a practical negotiation plan.

  • Specialized agents review positions, leverage, constraints, evidence, and commercial context.
  • They prepare talking points, concessions, fallback options, participant prep, and session plans.
  • The workflow flags gaps, risks, and decision points while lawyers keep control of final strategy.
