Cross-Examination Prep

Prepare a witness-specific cross plan from indexed matter documents.
How it works

Cross-Examination Prep uses a witness-specific agentic workflow. It reads the indexed matter record, identifies what this witness can and cannot safely prove, and produces a controlled cross plan for lawyer review.

Upload or index
  • witness statements, affidavits, examination-in-chief, and prior testimony
  • pleadings, issues, charges, claim documents, and replies
  • contracts, correspondence, invoices, policies, exhibits, and objective records
  • expert reports, investigation records, transcripts, and court or tribunal orders
  • prior inconsistent statements, omissions, settlement context, and any red-line instructions
Output
  • witness role, opponent theory, do-not-contest points, and contested points
  • admissions to obtain and contradiction bundles with source anchors
  • question-by-question cross tree with purpose, expected answer, evasion path, denial path, risk, and stop rule
  • questions to avoid, likely judge questions, opponent repair, closing use, and missing material
  • a print-ready one-page cross plan plus the full courtroom preparation report
